    User Guide

    1. Enable the extension from the Chrome toolbar.
    2. Refresh the active tab for complete integration.
    3. Toggle Math Paste with Ctrl+M for windows or Cmd + M for Mac
    4. Hover over the UI and select your notebook or format.
    5. Copy the rendered Math from the AI Chat from top to bottom.

    Swtiching Tabs

    1. After switching to a new tab, make sure to refresh the active tab for full integration.
    2. Then launch Math Paste with Ctrl+M for windows or Cmd + M for Mac
