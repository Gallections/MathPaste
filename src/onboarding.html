✨ What is Math Paste?

Math Paste is a lightweight Chrome Extension that intercepts copy events on AI chatbot pages and automatically converts KaTeX-rendered math equations into the format your notebook app expects — no manual reformatting needed.

Compatible AI Platforms

  • ChatGPT (all models)
  • Claude.AI
  • Microsoft Copilot

⚙️ How to Use

  1. Open a supported AI chatbot in Chrome. The floating Math Paste pill appears automatically in the top-right corner.
  2. Hover over the pill to open the format selector, then click your target format.
  3. Select and copy math from the chatbot response as you normally would.
  4. Paste into your notebook — the math is already formatted correctly.
  5. Press Alt+Shift+M to toggle the extension on or off at any time.

Copy Direction

Both top-to-bottom and left-to-right selections are supported. Top-to-bottom is the most reliable — use it when copying large blocks with multiple equations.

🔧 Troubleshooting

  • If the pill disappears, refresh the tab — it will reappear automatically.
  • If math isn't converting, make sure a format other than None is selected.
  • If the extension stops responding, reload it from chrome://extensions/ and refresh the page.
